Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about dairy investments

Dairy cattle investment works by allowing investors to purchase ownership in dairy cattle. Your investment funds the acquisition, care, and management of the cattle. As the cattle produce milk, the revenue generated is shared with investors as dividends. Our team of professionals handles all aspects of cattle management, allowing you to enjoy passive income without the complexities of farm operations.

Our dairy investments typically yield between 12-15% annual returns, depending on the investment tier chosen. Single calf investments average 12%, milking cow investments 14%, and dairy herd investments 15%. Returns are paid as regular dividends based on milk production, and the exact amount can vary based on market conditions and production efficiency.

Your investment is secured by the physical asset (the cattle) and comprehensive insurance policies that protect against livestock loss. Additionally, we maintain a reserve fund to ensure consistent dividend payments even during temporary production fluctuations. All investments are documented with legal contracts specifying your ownership rights and entitlements.

Yes! We organize quarterly farm visits for our investors. During these visits, you can see the facilities, meet the management team, and even see your specific cattle (for single cow investors). Herd investors can tour the entire operation. We also host annual VIP events for larger investors, providing a more in-depth experience of the dairy operations.

At the end of your investment term, you have several options: 1) Renew your investment for another term, 2) Upgrade to a higher investment tier, 3) Liquidate your investment and receive the principal plus any appreciation in the cattle's value. We provide a 30-day notice before term completion to discuss your preferences and process your chosen option.
